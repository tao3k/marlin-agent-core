** Landing Decision

Gerbil upstream is not a Rust crate. Marlin should not vendor or execute the
Gerbil runtime inside the kernel.

The Rust landing surface is the existing =marlin-gerbil-ir= and
=marlin-gerbil-scheme= boundary: Gerbil source emits typed artifacts; Rust
records the requested artifact kind, validates that the compiler emitted that
kind, and then validates or executes the typed IR.

** Next Work

  runtime contract is chosen.
- Keep upstream research checkouts under =.data=; CI and crate tests must not
  depend on that ignored directory.
